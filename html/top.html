Top-Rope

Top Rope climbing or top roping is a form of rock climbing where the climber is securely attachd to a climbing rope that runs through an anchor at the top of the wall and back down to the belayer on the ground. When a climber falls they simply hang from the rope at the point of their fall. Top Rope is thought of as one of the safest forms of rock climbing. it is mostly used by beginners in the sport. The proccess goes like this, the climber ties themselves into the rope using a figure eight knot and the belayer clips their belay device into the other side of the rope. As the climber moves up the wall the belayer "takes in" the slack in the rope.
